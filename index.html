Talent+

UNOPS's talent technology, and how the pieces fit together.

Talent+ is the name we use for UNOPS's talent technology. It is not itself a system. It is a family of systems, each serving a distinct purpose, that together support how UNOPS attracts, selects, and integrates talent.

Each component in Talent+ has a short name and a system name. The short name, like People or Careers, describes what the component is about and is how we will refer to things going forward. The system name, like Talent Workspace or Career Marketplace, identifies the platform you may already know.

What's changing
  • Launching Q4 2026
    People Talent Workspace joins the Talent+ family as the workspace where HR practitioners and hiring managers find, evaluate, and select talent. Internal candidates explore their next role here.
  • Retiring
    The Hiring Manager Portal is being retired. Hiring managers will work directly in People, with the right visibility for their role.
  • Continuing
    Careers Career Marketplace, Recruit HR Portal, and Join Onboarding Platform continue as they are. The family grows. The existing systems stay in place.
