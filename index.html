Talent+

UNOPS’ talent technology and how the pieces fit together.

Talent+ is the name we use for UNOPS’ talent technology. It is not itself a system. It is a family of systems, each serving a distinct purpose, that together support how UNOPS attracts, selects, and integrates talent.

To date, each component in Talent+ has been referred to by a technical name. Moving forward, each component will have a descriptive name, like People or Careers, that describes what the component is. This is how we will refer to the component moving forward. The technical names, like Talent Workspace or Career Marketplace, may also be used to help and be clear about which component we are referring to. We can use the names interchangeably, but important to establish clarity now because…

What's changing
  • Going forward

    Talent+ is UNOPS’ talent technology. It is made up of four components:

    • Careers - Career Marketplace
    • Recruit - HR Portal
    • Join - Onboarding Platform
    • People - Talent Workspace

    These are the names we will use going forward to refer to UNOPS’ talent technology.

  • Launching Q4 2026
    People Talent Workspace joins the Talent+ family as the workspace where HR practitioners and hiring managers find, evaluate, and select talent. Internal candidates explore their next role here.
  • Retiring
    The Hiring Manager Portal is being retired. Hiring managers will work directly in People, with the right visibility for their role.
  • Continuing
    Careers Career Marketplace, Recruit HR Portal, and Join Onboarding Platform continue as they are. The family grows. The existing systems stay in place.
